About

Our mission

Our mission is to provide universities with a reliable and efficient solution to credit card fraud detection, empowering them to safeguard their financial operations and protect their students and staff from financial loss and identity theft. We believe that AI technology can play a crucial role in achieving this mission, and we are committed to making it accessible and easy to use for all universities.

At CCard, we make credit card fraud detection a breeze with our AI-powered tools. We accept CSV files, analyze the data, and produce graphs and images of the results, making it easy to identify fraudulent transactions in your dataset.

Unleashing the Power of AI to Detect Credit Card Fraud

CCard is a company that specializes in identifying fraudulent and legit credit card transactions using state-of-the-art AI models. We accept CSV files containing credit card transaction data and analyze them to provide accurate results that you can trust.
